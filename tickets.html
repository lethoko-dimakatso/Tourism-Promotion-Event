Frequently Asked Questions

Are children allowed?

Yes! Children under 12 enter free when accompanied by an adult. Special kids' activities are available throughout the festival.

Is parking available?

Free parking is available on-site for all ticket holders. VIP ticket holders get reserved parking spaces.

Can I bring food?

Outside food and drinks are not permitted, but our food court offers a wide variety of traditional and international cuisine.

Is the venue wheelchair accessible?

Yes, the festival grounds are fully wheelchair accessible. Please inform us of any special requirements when booking.
