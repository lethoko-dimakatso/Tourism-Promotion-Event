Volunteer With Us

Join our team of passionate volunteers and be part of something special! We welcome volunteers from all backgrounds to help make the festival a success.

🎯 Volunteer Opportunities

  • Event Coordination
  • Guest Services

  • Free festival access
  • Certificate of appreciation
  • Networking opportunities
  • Cultural immersion experience
  • Volunteer t-shirt and meals
  • Letters of recommendation

To volunteer: Email us at volunteers@lesothofestival.ls with your CV and a brief statement about why you'd like to join our team.
