Festival Programme & Schedule

Join us for three days of cultural immersion, entertainment, and celebration! Our carefully curated programme showcases the best of Basotho culture through performances, workshops, exhibitions, and interactive experiences.

Special Features

🎭 Cultural Villages

Experience different Basotho cultural traditions in our themed villages, each representing different regions and communities of Lesotho.

🍽️ Food Festival

Savor authentic Basotho cuisine and international dishes prepared by renowned chefs and local home cooks.

🎪 Kids Zone

Special activities for children including face painting, cultural games, and educational workshops.

🛍️ Craft Market

Shop for authentic Basotho crafts, souvenirs, and artworks directly from local artisans.
